GDG BlrDroid's App Expo 2016

Register

Let the world know what you create with technology.

Android App Expo 2016 give developers a stage to showcase their innovations, for startups to meet the right people they are looking for and the organization to keep abreast with the new kids on the block. Aiming to bring the developers, startups and organizations, from all over the country together to find a common goal, GDG Bangalore and Bangalore Android User Group presents Android App Expo 2016.

Basic Evaluation

Basic evaluation criteria would include:

  • Completeness of the solution
  • Uniqueness of the app
  • Popularity and Rating
  • Availability across form factors
  • Adherence to UI guidelines
  • Number of Installs
  • Innovation and Impact

Round 1

Submitted apps will be filtered by GDGs. The top 20 apps will be invited to demo their apps at the expo.

Round 2

The best apps at the app expo will be judged by a panel of judges and by the popularity of their app in the public vote.
